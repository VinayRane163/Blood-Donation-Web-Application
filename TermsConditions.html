Terms and Conditions for Blood Donation

Welcome to our Blood Donation Program. By participating in our program, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.

1. Eligibility Criteria

To donate blood, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be at least 18 years old and not older than 65 years.
  • Weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • Be in good health and feel well on the day of donation.
  • Not have donated blood in the last 56 days.

2. Medical History

By agreeing to donate blood, you confirm that you do not have any medical conditions that could be contraindicated for blood donation. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Heart disease or high blood pressure.
  • Recent surgery or major illness.
  • Any infectious diseases such as HIV, Hepatitis B or C.

3. Privacy and Confidentiality

We are committed to protecting your personal information. All data collected during the donation process will be kept confidential and used only for the purposes of your donation and related medical follow-ups.

4. Rights and Responsibilities

As a blood donor, you have the right to:

  • Receive clear information about the donation process.
  • Withdraw from the donation process at any time without any consequence.

You are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate information regarding your health history.
  • Following any instructions provided by medical personnel.

5. Risks and Side Effects

Blood donation is a safe procedure, but like any medical procedure, it carries some risks. Possible side effects include dizziness, fainting, or bruising at the needle site. If you experience any adverse effects, please inform the staff immediately.

6. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to update or modify these terms and conditions at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and it is your responsibility to review them periodically.
